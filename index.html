Our Story

Built by a village, for the children of that village.

Busy Bees Foundation School opened its doors in 2013 with just 70 nursery pupils in Giheka Village, Kagugu Cell, Kinyinya Sector. Today more than 334 learners walk through our gates across a nursery section of 3 levels and a primary section of 6 classes - six promotions strong at the Primary Leaving Examination.

We are striving to offer quality education in conformity with Rwanda’s Competency-Based Curriculum, nurturing a holistic child who performs well academically, who loves and cares for those around them, and who works with their own hands, responsible for their actions.

“Education is a conduit for both intellectual and personal growth.”

Mrs. Berthe FURAHA K. - School Principal

Our Philosophy

Head, Heart and Hands - every learner builds all three.

We nurture a holistic child: one who performs well academically, who loves and cares for the people around them, and who uses their own hands to work, responsible for their acts.

01

Head

Intellectual growth through rigorous, competency-based academics across every core subject of the CBC.

02

Heart

Moral and emotional development anchored in our core values and a Christ-centered community life.

03

Hands

Practical, hands-on responsibility built through ICT, the arts, sport and everyday school life.

Academics

Rwanda’s Competency-Based Curriculum, taught with language excellence.

We teach every subject prescribed by the CBC, and go further: French is our medium of instruction from Nursery through Primary 2, while English takes over from Primary 3 upward - equipping every learner to communicate proficiently in both languages, alongside globally required ICT skills.
