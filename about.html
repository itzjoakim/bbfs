History & Background

Thirteen years of growth in Giheka Village.

Busy Bees Foundation School is a private school located in Giheka Village, Kagugu Cell, Kinyinya Sector, Gasabo District, Kigali City. The school opened its doors in 2013 with only 70 pupils at nursery level. Today, the school comprises two sections: the nursery section with 3 levels and the primary section with 6 classes. The sixth Primary 6 promotion has sat for the 2023 Primary Leaving Examination, and the total number of pupils now stands at 334 across both sections.

The school employs highly qualified staff and ensures continuous professional development. We strive to offer quality education in conformity with the current Rwandan educational curriculum (CBC). Our compelling aspiration is excellence in teaching and learning, and we are committed to playing an exceptional role in the national effort to sustain and grow a competitive Knowledge-Based Economy through effective implementation of the Competency-Based Curriculum.

In addition, we focus on nurturing a holistic child - Head, Heart and Hands - who performs well academically, who loves and cares about the people around them, and who uses their hands to work, responsible for their own actions.

Our Vision

To be a catalyst for sustainable development where all children feel loved, respected and encouraged to perform at their highest potential through quality and affordable education.

Our Mission

To empower each child to acquire, demonstrate and value knowledge and competencies that will enable them, as a lifelong learner, to contribute to the development of Rwanda and the world at large - with emphasis on culture and quality education in a Christ-centered environment, through the involvement of all stakeholders.

“Culture, Skills and Hard Work for Positive Transformation”

Our School Aim

Nurturing each child to their full potential.

We aim at nurturing, educating, inspiring and developing each child as a unique individual to their full potential, with much emphasis on both academic performance and character.

Our School Philosophy

Busy Bees Foundation School rests on the belief that its success comes from visionary leadership, highly skilled staff, constant professional development, innovation and teamwork - “no man is an island.”

BBFS does its best to attract, develop and retain a skilled workforce, and to encourage a consultative working atmosphere.

Our School Value Proposition

For all parents who want their children to excel in modern languages and ICT, Busy Bees Foundation School provides a special language and ICT programme that equips our learners, both in Nursery and Primary sections, with a lifelong capacity to communicate proficiently in French and English, at a globally required standard of ICT skills.

Message of the School Principal

Welcome to Busy Bees Foundation School.

As School Principal, it has always been my mission to provide quality education in a child-friendly environment that empowers both educators and learners to reach their full potential. With a deep-rooted passion for education, I am confident that together with our dedicated team and educational partners, we shall meet the needs of our community, fostering skills, attitudes and values that will transform our pupils into capable agents of positive change for our country’s rapid development.

I view education as a conduit for both intellectual and personal growth, aiming to cultivate well-rounded individuals who embody academic excellence and strong character in order to become future leaders. Parents, your child deserves the best - consider joining our vibrant school community and stay updated with our events through our website. Visitors, our doors are always open to you.

Mrs. Berthe FURAHA K.
BBFS School Principal
