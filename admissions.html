Admissions

A simple, personal process.

We welcome applications for Nursery 1–3 and Primary 1–6 year-round. Busy Bees Foundation School offers an affordable fee structure, a safe and child-friendly campus, and a staff that is qualified and continuously trained.

  1. 1
    Tell us about your child.

    Share their name, date of birth and the grade they’re applying for.

  2. 2
    Tell us about your family.

    Parent or guardian name, phone and email, and how you heard about us.

  3. 3
    We’ll be in touch.

    Our admissions team follows up to guide you through registration.
